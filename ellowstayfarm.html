Ellow Stay Farm

Escape to Nature: A Perfect Getaway at Yellow Stay Farm, Khopoli

Looking for a peaceful retreat away from the city's hustle? Yellow Stay Farm in Khopoli offers the perfect escape with a blend of luxury and nature. Surrounded by lush greenery, this farm stay provides a rejuvenating experience for families, friends, and corporate groups. With 7 AC bedrooms , an LCD screen TV , and a music system with karaoke mics , entertainment is always at your fingertips. The swimming pool (40x20 sq. ft.) and a relaxing bubble pool add to the charm, offering a refreshing dip under the open sky. Whether you want to enjoy a game of cricket on the turf , relax on the open lawn , or indulge in delicious veg and non-veg food prepared by an in-house cook , Yellow Stay Farm ensures a seamless and enjoyable stay. A caretaker is available to handle your needs, making your vacation stress-free.

Affordable Luxury: Stay & Dine at Yellow Stay Farm

Yellow Stay Farm offers an all-inclusive stay and food package at reasonable rates. Weekday stays cost ₹2000 per head , while weekend stays are ₹2800 per head , with a minimum booking requirement of 20 people on weekends. This makes it a fantastic choice for group trips, family gatherings, and celebrations. Guests can enjoy a variety of delicious vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals freshly prepared on-site. The farm’s serene environment, spacious rooms, and comfortable amenities ensure a home-like feel, while the geyser facilities provide a relaxing hot shower after a fun-filled day. Whether you're planning a short getaway or an extended vacation, Yellow Stay Farm delivers comfort and value in one package.

Customize Your Experience with Add-On Facilities

Want to enhance your experience? Yellow Stay Farm offers additional services at an extra cost to make your trip even more special. Enjoy a barbecue starter under the stars or sip on refreshing cold drinks while lounging by the pool. For those arriving by train, a pick-and-drop service from Khopoli Station is available, ensuring a hassle-free journey. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a reunion, or just seeking a weekend retreat, these extra perks allow you to tailor your stay to your preferences. From adventure to relaxation, Yellow Stay Farm is your go-to destination for an unforgettable experience.
