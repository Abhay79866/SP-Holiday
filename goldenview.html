Golden Villa Lonavala

Golden Villa, Lonavala: A Luxurious Retreat for Every Occasion

Golden Villa in Lonavala is the perfect getaway for those looking for a relaxing yet fun-filled vacation. Nestled in the scenic beauty of Lonavala, this villa offers a serene atmosphere with top-notch amenities. Whether you are planning a trip with friends or a family vacation, Golden Villa provides a comfortable and enjoyable stay. With a swimming pool measuring 20x20 sq. ft, an open lawn for outdoor activities, and four spacious air-conditioned bedrooms, this villa ensures a luxurious experience. The well-furnished hall, equipped with a TV and a music system, makes it an ideal place for entertainment. Guests can enjoy singing sessions with the karaoke mic, making their evenings lively and memorable. The villa also provides a caretaker and a cook, ensuring that you have a hassle-free stay. Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options are available, making it a delightful retreat for all kinds of food lovers.

Affordable Luxury: Stay and Food Included at Golden Villa

One of the best features of Golden Villa is its affordability without compromising on luxury. The pricing is designed to cater to different budgets, with weekday stays costing ₹2,000 per person and weekend stays priced at ₹2,800 per person, inclusive of stay and food. For weekends, there is a minimum guarantee of 15 people, making it an excellent option for group getaways. The villa is well-maintained, offering modern facilities like hot water geysers in bathrooms and a comfortable swing in the open lawn, where guests can relax and enjoy the natural beauty. With its cozy ambiance and well-equipped amenities, Golden Villa ensures that every guest has a memorable and comfortable experience. Whether you are planning a corporate retreat, a birthday party, or a casual weekend getaway, this villa is a great choice for a relaxing vacation.

Additional Services to Enhance Your Stay at Golden Villa

Golden Villa offers additional services at an extra cost to make your stay even more convenient and enjoyable. For those traveling from out of town, the villa provides pick-and-drop services from Lonavala Station, ensuring a smooth journey to the property. Guests can also enjoy a barbecue session, where delicious starters can be prepared for an outdoor dining experience. To keep the party going, cold drinks and refreshments are available upon request. Whether you are looking for a peaceful retreat or a lively celebration, Golden Villa caters to all your needs. With its scenic location, premium facilities, and excellent service, this villa is an ideal destination for a memorable holiday. So, gather your group, pack your bags, and head to Golden Villa in Lonavala for an unforgettable stay!
