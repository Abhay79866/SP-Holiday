Silver Wave Villa Boisar

Experience Luxury and Comfort at Silver Wave Villa, Boisar

Nestled in the serene coastal town of Boisar, Silver Wave Villa is the perfect getaway for those looking to unwind in luxury. With a beach-facing view, this villa offers a breathtaking experience that blends nature with comfort. The property features four air-conditioned bedrooms, ensuring a cool and relaxing stay. The spacious hall and open lawn provide ample space for group activities, making it an ideal retreat for families, friends, and corporate gatherings. Adding to the charm is a 30 x 20 sq. ft. swimming pool, where guests can take a refreshing dip while enjoying the sea breeze. The villa also comes equipped with a TV, geyser, music system, and a karaoke mic, allowing guests to create memorable moments filled with fun and entertainment. Whether you're planning a quiet escape or a lively celebration, Silver Wave Villa caters to all your needs with its homely yet luxurious ambiance.

Affordable Stay with Delicious Food and Premium Facilities

Silver Wave Villa offers an all-inclusive stay and food package, making your trip hassle-free. Guests can enjoy a weekday getaway at just ₹2,000 per head, while weekends are available for ₹2,800 per head. The package includes delicious veg and non-veg meals, prepared with a homely touch. For seafood lovers, the villa also offers fresh seafood options at an additional cost. A minimum of 18 guests is required for weekend bookings, ensuring a vibrant and social atmosphere. Along with the basic amenities, guests can opt for a barbecue starter at an extra charge, perfect for grilling and enjoying under the stars. Whether you're savoring a wholesome meal indoors or enjoying a beachside feast, the culinary experience at Silver Wave Villa is sure to leave a lasting impression.

A Perfect Destination for Relaxation and Fun Activities

Silver Wave Villa isn't just about a peaceful retreat—it's a hub for fun and relaxation. The swing in the lawn, the music system for party vibes, and the karaoke mic for singing sessions make it an exciting destination for all age groups. The caretaker service ensures that guests have a comfortable and seamless stay. Whether you're soaking in the beach view, enjoying a swim in the pool, or unwinding in the well-furnished hall, every corner of this villa is designed to enhance your vacation experience. The villa’s proximity to the beach allows guests to take peaceful walks, enjoy the sunset, or simply relax with the sound of waves. Be it a weekend getaway, a birthday celebration, or a corporate outing, Silver Wave Villa in Boisar offers the perfect blend of nature, luxury, and entertainment.
